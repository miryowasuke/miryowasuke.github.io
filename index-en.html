Chirastock eliminates "Do we still have it?" —
a family inventory management app.

Detergent, toilet paper, food stock — household items are surprisingly hard to track. Chirastock lets you check at a glance with 3-level color coding and share with family. Fewer forgotten and duplicate purchases mean easier shopping.
